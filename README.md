# RecipEZ
RecipEZ is an android app that brings all your recipes into one easy to use organizational tool.
Simply take a photo of your recipe to import the ingredients and instructions.
Or, copy and paste the URL of your favorite recipe website and let RecipEZ do all the work.
Add or edit notes, photos, and more to make your recipes work best for you.
Easily convert measuring units, add ingredients to shopping lists, create a pantry of ingredients you own, easily share recipes with friends, and so much more.
